Characters


Crisostomo Ibarra
His full name is Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin. He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra. He studied in Spain for 7 years. He was tall and you can see from his face that he is part Spanish. A lot were questioning and interested in him due to him not being home for 7 years.


Rafael Ibarra
He is Crisostomo's dad. He was charged of the death of an "Artilyero". He was even called "erehe at pilibustero" (meaning: stubborn and rebel). Unfortunately, he was arrested and those time he had experience deppresion and because of that, he commited suicide.
